OnePaywall integrates with the following services:

| Integration | Purpose | Required for |
|-------------|---------|-------------|
| [Razorpay](/docs/integrations/razorpay) | Payment processing | Subscriptions & article unlocks |
| [Google AdSense](/docs/integrations/adsense) | Ad serving | Network ad steps in gates |
| [Google Ad Manager](/docs/integrations/google-ad-manager) | Programmatic ad serving | Network ad steps in gates |
| [Resend](/docs/integrations/resend) | Email sending | Campaigns & automations |
| [Webhooks](/docs/integrations/webhooks) | Event notifications | Syncing with external systems |

## Where to configure

All integrations are configured in **Dashboard → Settings**:

- **Payment gateway** — Razorpay
- **Ad networks** — AdSense and GAM
- **Email settings** — Resend

Webhooks are configured under **Dashboard → Settings → Webhooks**.
