The **Subscribers** page shows every reader who has subscribed to one of your plans.

## Subscriber list

Go to **Dashboard → Subscribers** to see all subscribers. The list shows:

- **Email** — the subscriber's email address
- **Plan** — which subscription plan they're on
- **Status** — active, paused, or cancelled
- **Started** — when the subscription began
- **Next billing date** — when their next payment is due

## Filtering and searching

Use the search bar to find a subscriber by email. Filter by status (active, paused, cancelled) or by plan.

## Subscriber actions

Click any subscriber row to open their details. You can:

- **Pause** — suspend billing temporarily (the subscriber retains access until the current period ends)
- **Cancel** — cancel the subscription at the end of the current billing period
- **Cancel immediately** — revoke access now and stop billing
- **Reactivate** — resume a paused or cancelled subscription

## Bulk operations

Select multiple subscribers using the checkboxes and apply bulk actions:

- **Export CSV** — download the selected subscribers as a CSV file
- **Cancel** — cancel selected subscriptions in bulk

## Subscription lifecycle

```
Active → Paused → Reactivated
      ↓
   Cancelled (end of period)
      ↓
   Expired
```

Cancelled subscribers retain access until their current billing period ends. After that, they see the gate again.
