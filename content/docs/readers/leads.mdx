title: Leads
description: Manage readers who gave their email in exchange for free access via a lead-capture gate step.
---

**Leads** are readers who submitted their email address through a lead-capture gate step. They haven't paid, but they've expressed interest — making them warm prospects for subscription conversion.

## Leads list

Go to **Dashboard → Subscribers → Leads** to see all captured leads. The list shows:

- **Email** — the lead's email address
- **Source** — which gate and step captured them
- **Captured** — when the lead was collected
- **Tags** — any tags you've added
- **Notes** — your internal notes

## Filtering leads

Filter by:
- **Source** — which domain or gate they came from
- **Date range** — when they were captured
- **Tags** — custom labels you've applied

## Tags and notes

Click a lead to open their record. You can:

- Add or remove **tags** (e.g. "high-intent", "tech", "newsletter")
- Write internal **notes** about the lead
- See the reader's history — which pages they visited and which gate they triggered

## Exporting leads

Click **Export CSV** to download your full leads list or a filtered subset. The CSV includes email, source, capture date, tags, and notes.

## Converting leads to subscribers

Leads are not automatically enrolled in your email list. To convert leads into email subscribers or track them in a CRM, export the CSV and import it into your email platform. Or set up an automation under **Dashboard → Email → Automations** to send a welcome campaign to new leads.
