Subscription plans define what readers pay for ongoing access to your site. You can offer multiple tiers (e.g. monthly and annual, or basic and premium).

<img src="/docs-images/pricing-plans.svg" alt="Pricing page showing Basic, Premium, and Enterprise plan cards with subscriber counts" style={{width:"100%",borderRadius:8,border:"1px solid #e5e5e5",marginTop:12,marginBottom:12}} />

## Creating a plan

1. Go to **Dashboard → Pricing**
2. Click **New plan**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — shown to readers (e.g. "Monthly", "Annual", "Premium")
   - **Price** — in your configured currency
   - **Billing period** — monthly or annual
   - **Description** — a short line shown in the gate overlay
4. Click **Save**

Plans are immediately available to be shown in Subscription gate steps.

## Managing plans

From the **Pricing** page you can:

- **Edit** a plan's name, description, or price
- **Archive** a plan — existing subscribers on the plan are not affected, but new readers won't see it
- **Set a free trial** — offer N days free before the first charge

## Plan display in the gate

When a Subscription step fires, readers see a list of your active plans. The plan name, price, and description are shown. Readers choose a plan and pay via your connected payment gateway.

## Currency

Your billing currency is set under **Dashboard → Settings → General**. All plans use this currency.

## Coupons and discounts

Coupons are not yet supported. To offer a discounted rate, create a separate plan at the discounted price and make it time-limited by archiving it after the promotion ends.
