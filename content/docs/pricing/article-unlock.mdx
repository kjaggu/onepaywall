---
title: Article Unlock Pricing
description: Let readers pay once to access a single article — without a full subscription.
---

Article unlocks let readers pay a one-time fee for access to a specific URL. This is useful for casual readers who aren't ready to subscribe.

## Setting up article unlock pricing

1. Go to **Dashboard → Pricing → Article Unlocks**
2. Set a **default price** — this applies to all URLs unless overridden
3. Optionally, add **per-URL prices** for specific articles

## Per-URL pricing

To set a different price for a specific article:

1. Click **Add URL price**
2. Enter the article URL (exact path, e.g. `/articles/my-premium-report`)
3. Enter the price for that article
4. Click **Save**

Per-URL prices override the default. Use them for high-value content like research reports or exclusive interviews.

## Using article unlock in a gate

Add an **Article unlock** step to your gate (see [Gate steps](/docs/gates/gate-steps)). When this step fires, the reader sees the unlock price for the current URL and can pay immediately.

After a successful payment, the unlock is stored against the reader's fingerprint. They don't pay again if they return to the same URL later.

## Access duration

Article unlocks grant permanent access to the specific URL. There is no expiry — once unlocked, always unlocked.

## Revenue from unlocks

Article unlock payments appear in **Dashboard → Revenue** and are included in the daily/monthly revenue totals on your analytics dashboard.
