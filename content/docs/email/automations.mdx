Automations are emails triggered by reader actions or inactivity. Set them up once and they run in the background without manual intervention.

## Available triggers

| Trigger | When it fires |
|---------|---------------|
| **New subscriber** | When a reader completes a subscription payment |
| **Segment entered** | When a reader's segment changes (e.g. casual → regular) |
| **Ad engaged** | When a reader completes an ad view |
| **Inactivity** | When a subscriber hasn't visited in N days |

## Creating an automation

1. Go to **Dashboard → Email → Automations**
2. Click **New automation**
3. Choose a **trigger**
4. Set trigger conditions (e.g. "subscriber on Monthly plan" or "inactive for 30 days")
5. Write the **email** — subject, body, from address
6. Set a **delay** (optional) — wait N hours/days after the trigger before sending
7. Toggle the automation **on** and click **Save**

## Recommended automations

### Welcome email

**Trigger:** New subscriber  
**Delay:** Immediate  
**Purpose:** Thank the subscriber, remind them what they get, link to your best content

### Re-engagement

**Trigger:** Inactivity (30 days)  
**Purpose:** Remind dormant subscribers why they subscribed. Include a link to recent popular content

### Upgrade prompt

**Trigger:** Segment entered (regular)  
**Purpose:** When a reader becomes a regular, prompt them to subscribe — they've demonstrated enough engagement to be likely to convert

## Automation stats

Each automation shows the total emails sent, open rate, and click rate over its lifetime. Use this to evaluate whether the automation is working and refine your copy.
