OnePaywall includes a built-in email sender powered by [Resend](https://resend.com). Connect your Resend API key to start sending campaigns and automations to your audience.

## What you can do

- **Campaigns** — one-off broadcast emails to your subscriber list or a segment
- **Automations** — trigger-based emails sent automatically when a reader takes an action

## Email hub

Go to **Dashboard → Email** to see:

- **Total subscribers** in your email list
- **Last campaign** stats (sent, open rate, click rate)
- **Active automations** count

## Setup required

Before sending email, you need to:

1. Connect a Resend API key — see [Email settings](/docs/email/email-settings)
2. Verify your sending domain — Resend requires DNS records (DKIM/SPF) to be set
3. Add your verified domain under **Email settings**

Without a verified domain, emails will either not send or land in spam.
