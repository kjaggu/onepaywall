Campaigns are one-off emails sent to your audience. Use them for newsletters, product announcements, exclusive offers, or any broadcast communication.

## Creating a campaign

1. Go to **Dashboard → Email → Campaigns**
2. Click **New campaign**
3. Fill in:
   - **Subject** — the email subject line
   - **Preview text** — the short text shown in inbox previews
   - **From name** and **From email** — must use your verified sending domain
   - **Audience** — choose who receives this campaign (see below)
   - **Body** — write your email content (rich text editor)
4. Click **Save draft** or **Send** / **Schedule**

## Audience targeting

Choose who receives your campaign:

| Audience | Who receives it |
|----------|-----------------|
| All subscribers | Every active subscriber |
| All leads | Every captured lead |
| Subscribers on a specific plan | Only readers on a chosen subscription plan |
| Segment | Readers matching a reader segment (new, casual, regular, power user) |

## Scheduling

You can send immediately or schedule for a future date and time. Scheduled campaigns can be cancelled before the send time.

## Campaign stats

After sending, each campaign shows:
- **Delivered** — emails successfully delivered
- **Opens** — unique opens (tracked via a 1×1 pixel)
- **Clicks** — unique link clicks
- **Bounces** — undeliverable addresses
- **Unsubscribes** — readers who opted out

## Unsubscribes

OnePaywall automatically adds an unsubscribe link to every campaign. Readers who click it are removed from your email list and will not receive future campaigns or automations.
