If you have multiple domains connected to OnePaywall, domain analytics lets you see performance data for each one separately.

## Accessing domain analytics

Go to **Dashboard → Analytics → [domain name]** to see the analytics for a specific domain.

Or, on the main analytics overview, click any domain in the domain breakdown table.

## Metrics per domain

- **Revenue** — total revenue from this domain over the selected period
- **Impressions** — gate shows on this domain
- **Conversion rate** — specific to this domain's readers and gate configuration
- **Top content** — the most visited gated URLs on this domain

## Top content

The **Top content** table shows which specific URLs are being gated most frequently and converting best. Use this to:

- Identify your best-performing premium content
- Spot high-traffic URLs with low conversion rates — consider changing their gate configuration
- Find content that readers are willing to pay for vs. content they abandon

## Comparing domains

Use the main analytics overview to compare domains side-by-side. Differences in conversion rate between domains often reflect differences in reader intent (niche content converts better) or gate configuration.

## Date ranges

Select from **7 days**, **30 days**, or **90 days**, or use the date picker for a custom range.
