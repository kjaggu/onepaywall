Go to **Dashboard → Analytics → Content** for article-level performance data and reader journey analysis.

## Content performance table

The table shows every gated URL with:

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **URL** | The gated page path |
| **Impressions** | Times the gate fired on this URL |
| **Conversions** | Readers who paid or converted |
| **Conversion rate** | Conversions ÷ Impressions |
| **Revenue** | Total revenue attributed to this URL |
| **Avg. read depth** | How far into the article readers scrolled before hitting the gate |

## Reader journey funnel

The funnel shows where readers drop off in the gate flow:

```
Landed on page
   ↓
Gate fired (impression)
   ↓
Saw subscription offer
   ↓
Clicked to pay
   ↓
Completed payment (conversion)
```

Large drop-offs at the "Clicked to pay" step suggest the price is too high. Large drop-offs at "Saw subscription offer" suggest the offer copy needs work.

## Source attribution

The source breakdown shows which traffic sources are driving the most paying readers:

- **Direct** — readers who typed or bookmarked your URL
- **Search** — readers arriving from Google or Bing
- **Social** — readers from social media links
- **Referral** — readers from other websites

Search traffic typically converts at a lower rate than direct or social traffic — readers from search often have less existing loyalty.

## Filtering

Filter the content table by domain, gate, or date range to narrow down your analysis.
