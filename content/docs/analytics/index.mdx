Go to **Dashboard → Analytics** for a high-level view of your monetization performance.

## Key metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Monthly revenue** | Total revenue in the current calendar month |
| **Active subscribers** | Readers with a currently active paid subscription |
| **Conversion rate** | Gate impressions that resulted in a subscription or unlock |
| **Gate impressions** | Total number of times a gate was shown to a reader |

## Revenue breakdown

The revenue chart shows daily or monthly revenue broken down by:
- **Subscriptions** — recurring subscription payments
- **Article unlocks** — one-time article unlock payments

Toggle between **7 days**, **30 days**, and **90 days** to see different time windows.

## Subscriber growth

The subscriber growth chart shows cumulative active subscribers over time. A rising trend means you're adding subscribers faster than you're losing them.

Churn is shown separately — the number of subscribers who cancelled in each period.

## Gate performance table

Below the charts, a table shows each of your gates with:
- Impressions
- Conversions
- Conversion rate
- Revenue attributed to that gate

Use this to identify which gates are performing well and which need adjustment.

## Hourly activity

The overview shows an hourly heatmap of gate impressions for the current day. Use this to understand when your readers are most active.
