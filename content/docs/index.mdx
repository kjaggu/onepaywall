OnePaywall turns reader traffic into revenue using adaptive paywalls, subscriptions, advertising, and email — all from a single embed snippet.

## What you can do

- **Gate content** — Show subscription prompts, ads, or lead-capture forms to readers who haven't paid
- **Sell subscriptions** — Charge readers monthly or annually for full access to your site
- **Sell article unlocks** — Let readers pay per article without a full subscription
- **Serve ads** — Monetize readers who won't subscribe by showing display or video ads as a gate step
- **Capture leads** — Collect emails from readers in exchange for free access
- **Sell digital products** — Offer downloadable files (e-books, reports, templates) directly to readers
- **Send email campaigns** — Reach your subscriber list with newsletters and automated flows

## How it works

1. **Add your domain** — Register your site and get a site key
2. **Install the embed snippet** — One script tag in your HTML `<head>`
3. **Create a gate** — Define which URLs are gated and what readers see
4. **Configure pricing** — Set subscription plans or per-article prices
5. **Connect integrations** — Link your payment processor, ad networks, and email provider

OnePaywall never touches your server — the embed script runs in the browser and all decisions are made server-side via the CDN edge.
