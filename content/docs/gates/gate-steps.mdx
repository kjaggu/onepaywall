A gate can contain multiple steps. When a reader hits the gate, they see the first step. If they close or decline it, they see the next step (if configured).

<img src="/docs-images/gate-steps.svg" alt="Gate step type selector showing subscription, article unlock, ad, and lead capture options, plus an example step sequence" style={{width:"100%",borderRadius:8,border:"1px solid #e5e5e5",marginTop:12,marginBottom:12}} />

## Subscription step

Shows a subscription offer. The reader can choose from the plans you've created under **Pricing**.

**Configuration options:**
- Choose which plans to display (monthly, annual, or both)
- Customize the headline and description shown to readers
- Set a free trial period (optional)

The reader pays through your connected payment gateway (see [Razorpay setup](/docs/integrations/razorpay)). On successful payment, the gate lifts immediately.

## Article unlock step

Lets the reader pay once to access a single article, without a subscription.

**Configuration options:**
- Set a per-article price (or inherit from your global article-unlock pricing)
- Customize the unlock prompt copy

Article unlocks are stored per-reader so they don't need to pay again if they return to the same URL.

## Ad step

Shows a video or display ad. The reader watches/views the ad in exchange for time-limited access to the content.

**Configuration options:**
- Select which ad units to show (configured under **Advertising**)
- Set access duration after a completed ad view (e.g. 24 hours, 7 days)

Readers must complete the ad (reach the end of a video, or meet the minimum display time) to receive access.

## Lead capture step

Prompts the reader to submit their email address. On submission, they get free access.

**Configuration options:**
- Customize the prompt headline and body text
- Set what the reader receives: time-limited access, permanent access, or a "thank you" redirect

Captured leads appear under **Dashboard → Subscribers → Leads**.

## Digital product step

Prompts the reader to purchase a downloadable file you've uploaded (e-book, report, template, etc.).

**Configuration options:**
- Choose which digital product to promote
- Customize the prompt copy and thumbnail

After purchase, the reader receives a download link and gets access to the gated content.

## Step order and fallbacks

Steps fire in order. If a reader closes Step 1, they see Step 2 on the next page visit. This lets you build graceful fallback sequences:

```
Step 1: Subscription  →  If declined...
Step 2: Article unlock  →  If declined...
Step 3: Ad  →  If declined...
Step 4: Lead capture
```

Readers who complete any step at any point get access to the gated content.
