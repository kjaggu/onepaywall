## Step 1 — Open the gate builder

Go to **Dashboard → Gates → New gate**.

Give your gate a descriptive name (e.g. "Premium articles paywall" or "Newsletter gate").

<img src="/docs-images/gate-builder.svg" alt="Gate builder showing name, domain, triggers, and step configuration" style={{width:"100%",borderRadius:8,border:"1px solid #e5e5e5",marginTop:12,marginBottom:12}} />

## Step 2 — Set up triggers

Triggers define which URLs activate this gate. Go to the **Triggers** tab.

Add one or more URL rules:

| Rule type | Example | What it matches |
|-----------|---------|-----------------|
| Exact path | `/articles/my-post` | Only that exact URL |
| Path prefix | `/articles/*` | All URLs starting with `/articles/` |
| Domain-wide | `*` | Every page on the domain |

See [Triggers](/docs/gates/triggers) for advanced pattern syntax.

## Step 3 — Add gate steps

Go to the **Steps** tab. Steps define what the reader sees when the gate fires. You can add multiple steps — readers progress through them if they decline earlier ones.

Available step types:

- **Subscription** — show a subscription offer with your pricing plans
- **Article unlock** — let the reader pay for just this article
- **Ad** — show a video or display ad as the price of access
- **Lead capture** — ask for an email address in exchange for free access
- **Digital product** — prompt the reader to purchase a downloadable file

See [Gate steps](/docs/gates/gate-steps) for details on configuring each type.

## Step 4 — Save and publish

Click **Save**. The gate is now live and will fire for any URL matching your trigger rules.

To pause a gate without deleting it, open the gate and toggle it off.

## Tips

- Start with a single **Subscription** step to test conversion before adding fallback steps
- Use **Ad** as a fallback step for readers who are unlikely to subscribe — it keeps them monetised
- Add a **Lead capture** step last to grow your email list from readers who won't pay at all
