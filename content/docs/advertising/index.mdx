Ad units let you monetize readers who won't pay for a subscription by showing them an ad in exchange for access to your content.

## How advertising works in gates

When you add an **Ad** step to a gate, readers who reach that step are shown an ad. After they view or complete the ad, they receive temporary access to the gated content.

Ad units can come from:
- **Direct ads** — ad creatives you upload yourself
- **Ad networks** — served by Google AdSense or Google Ad Manager via your connected credentials

## Creating an ad unit

1. Go to **Dashboard → Advertising**
2. Click **New ad unit**
3. Choose the source:
   - **Direct** — upload a creative (image or video) and enter a click-through URL
   - **Network** — select a connected ad network (AdSense or GAM)
4. Set the ad dimensions and placement
5. Click **Save**

## Ad unit types

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Display | Static or animated image, shown full-screen or as an overlay |
| Video | Pre-roll style video that readers must watch to completion |

Video ads have higher CPMs and are recommended as the primary ad type in gate steps.

## Using an ad unit in a gate

Add an **Ad** step to your gate and select which ad units to include. See [Gate steps](/docs/gates/gate-steps) for details.

## Ad creative guidelines

- **Image ads**: minimum 600×400 px, JPG or PNG, under 500 KB
- **Video ads**: MP4, H.264, minimum 720p, under 50 MB, 15–60 seconds
