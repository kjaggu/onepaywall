---
title: Ad Analytics
description: Track impressions, completion rates, fill rate, and revenue from your gate ad units.
---

Go to **Dashboard → Advertising → Analytics** to see performance data for all your ad units.

## Key metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Impressions** | Total number of times an ad was shown |
| **Completions** | Readers who completed the ad (watched to end for video, met minimum time for display) |
| **Completion rate** | Completions ÷ Impressions |
| **Fill rate** | % of ad requests that resulted in an ad being served (network ads only) |
| **Revenue** | Estimated revenue from network ads (based on CPM/CPC reporting from the network) |

## Filtering

Filter by:
- **Date range** — last 7, 30, or 90 days, or a custom range
- **Ad unit** — compare performance across individual units
- **Domain** — see how ads perform on each of your sites
- **Reader segment** — see completion rates by reader type (new, casual, regular, power user)

## Completion rate by category

The analytics page shows ad completion rates broken down by content category. This helps you understand which topics have readers most willing to watch ads — useful for deciding which gates to add ad steps to.

## Optimising ad performance

- **Low fill rate** on network ads: check your GAM or AdSense settings — the ad unit may have targeting restrictions or floor prices that are preventing bids
- **Low completion rate**: try shorter video ads (15s performs better than 30s) or switch to display ads for segments with low patience
- **High completion rate but no subscriptions**: this segment is monetisable via ads — consider keeping an ad step permanently in the gate for these readers
