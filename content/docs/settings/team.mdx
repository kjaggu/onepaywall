---
title: Team
description: Invite team members and manage their access to your OnePaywall dashboard.
---

Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Team** to manage who has access to your publisher account.

## Inviting a team member

1. Click **Invite member**
2. Enter their email address
3. Select a role (see below)
4. Click **Send invite**

The invited person receives an email with a link to accept the invitation. They'll need to create a OnePaywall account (or log in if they already have one) to gain access.

## Roles

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Admin** | Full access — manage gates, subscribers, billing, and team |
| **Editor** | Create and edit gates, manage content settings; cannot manage billing or team |
| **Viewer** | Read-only access to analytics and subscriber data |

## Removing a team member

Click the three-dot menu next to a member and select **Remove**. Their access is revoked immediately.

## Pending invitations

Invitations that haven't been accepted appear in a **Pending** section. You can resend or cancel them.
