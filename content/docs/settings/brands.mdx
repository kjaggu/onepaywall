title: Brands
description: Create multiple brand profiles to manage different publications or products under one account.
---

Brands let you manage multiple distinct publications under a single OnePaywall account. Each brand has its own name, logo, and set of domains.

## When to use brands

Use brands when you run more than one publication that should appear separate to readers. For example:

- A tech blog and a finance newsletter under the same publisher account
- Multiple regional editions of the same publication
- A main publication and a separate premium newsletter

## Creating a brand

1. Go to **Dashboard → Brands**
2. Click **New brand**
3. Enter:
   - **Brand name** — shown to readers in gate overlays and emails
   - **Logo** — displayed in gate overlays (optional)
4. Click **Save**

## Assigning domains to a brand

When adding or editing a domain (see [Domains](/docs/settings/domains)), you can assign it to a brand. The brand's name and logo will be used in gate overlays for that domain.

## Brand-level settings

Each brand can have:
- Its own subscription plans
- Its own gate configurations
- Separate analytics

This lets you keep revenue and subscriber data separate between publications.
