---
title: Billing
description: Manage your OnePaywall subscription plan and payment method.
---

Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Billing** to manage your own OnePaywall subscription — this is separate from the reader payments you collect.

## OnePaywall plans

OnePaywall charges you a platform fee based on the plan you're on. Plans differ by:

- **Monthly active readers** limit
- **Domains** allowed
- **Features** available (advanced analytics, multiple brands, etc.)

Current plan details and pricing are shown on the Billing page.

## Changing your plan

Click **Change plan** to upgrade or downgrade. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

## Payment method

Your OnePaywall subscription is billed to the card on file. Click **Update payment method** to change your card.

## Invoices

Monthly invoices are available under **Dashboard → Invoices**. Each invoice shows the billing period, plan, and amount charged. Click any invoice to download a PDF.

## Cancelling your OnePaywall account

To cancel your OnePaywall subscription, go to **Dashboard → Settings → Danger Zone**. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
