---
title: General Settings
description: Configure your publisher name, currency, and timezone.
---

Go to **Dashboard → Settings → General** to update your account's basic settings.

## Publisher name

Your publisher name is shown to readers in subscription confirmations and emails. Use the name your readers know you by — your publication or brand name.

## Currency

Select the currency for all reader-facing prices — subscription plans, article unlocks, and digital products. Razorpay must support this currency for your account's country.

Changing currency after you have active subscribers is not recommended — existing subscriptions will continue to be charged in the original currency.

## Timezone

Set your timezone so that analytics reports, email schedule times, and revenue summaries align with your working hours. This does not affect payment processing — all payments are processed in UTC.

## Saving changes

Click **Save** to apply changes. Currency changes take effect for new subscriptions only.
